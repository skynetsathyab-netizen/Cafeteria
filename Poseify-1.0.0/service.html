VEG

Veg (Vegetarian) means food made only from plant sources like vegetables, fruits, grains, pulses, nuts, and dairy. It does not include meat, fish, or eggs. Veg food is healthy, easy to digest, and widely followed in India.

NON VEG

Non-veg (Non-vegetarian) means food that includes meat, fish, chicken, or eggs. It is rich in protein and helps in body growth and strength. Many people eat non-veg for energy and taste.

Snacks are light foods eaten between meals. They are quick to prepare and easy to eat. Examples include biscuits, chips, samosa, fruits, and nuts.
